# Research Material Summary

This directory contains project notes and local methodology references used while building the CSCV/PBO validation framework.

The executable research pipeline and generated report are in:

- `scripts/run_cscv_pipeline.py`
- `results/report.md`

The project follows the CSCV/PBO methodology associated with Bailey, Borwein, López de Prado, and Zhu, and applies it to 5-minute T/TL Chinese Government Bond Futures strategy parameter validation.
